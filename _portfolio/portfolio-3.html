Testing was conducted for fluctuated air and water flow rates entering the test section from cross-flow and cell impingement interfaces, and two-phase flow regimes were identified through a high-speed camera. Through image-based analysis, the flow-rate ratio was tuned to observe the progression of critical flow regimes for optimal cooling efficiency.
